How To Play:

1.Guess a random letter.
2.If the letter is in the word, you can continue playing. Else, one chance is gone.
3.The player will win the game if he guess the word right.There is a total of 10 levels.
4.The game will automatically end if all chances are used.


Score:

1.Guessing each word right gives you points.
2.If you lose the game, 0 points will be rewarded.
